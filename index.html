How it Works

Technology that feels like magic.

Behind it: pure precision.

1

Smart collars for every dog

Every dog in our care wears a lightweight smart collar with a mini camera, Bluetooth chip and speaker. The camera faces forward — filming what the dog sees, which means the other dogs around them.

2

Wall cameras in the Dog-Sitter House

Fixed HD cameras on every wall cover every corner of the house and garden. Together with the collar cameras, they create a seamless, gap-free picture.

3

AI-powered dog recognition

Our AI identifies each dog in real time and automatically selects the best available camera feed for your dog — contextually and with precision.

4

Privacy by Design

All people in the frame are automatically pixelated and anonymised in real time. No member of staff is ever identifiable. Privacy first.
